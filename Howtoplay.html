How to play 15 puzzle game




OBJECTIVE
The goal of this game is to arrange the shuffled tiles in the correct order to recreate the original image or
numeric sequence. The tiles should be in order from left to right, top to bottom, with the buttom-
right corner left blank.
1.         How to Move tiles
*       Click or tap on a tile adjacent to the blank space to slide it into the empty slot
*       Only tiles that are directly next to the blank space(up, down, left or right) can be moved
2.         Winning the Game
*       Once all tiles arearranged in the correct order, the game will display a "Congrats! you have finished the puzzle"
messsage
3.       Tips
*       Plan your moves carefully to avoid unnecesary backtracking
*       Start solving the top-left corner and work row by row for a systematic approach
